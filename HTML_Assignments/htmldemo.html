About MACS

MACS College is one of the best colleges of Pune and is affiliated to University of Pune. The College has been accredited by NAAC with ‘A’ grade in its first Assessment in 2012. It is the leading college offering various graduation & post-graduation courses which caters to the needs of local, national and international students. The college with state of the art infrastructure and ultra-modern laboratories, healthy learning environment and academic ambience with experienced & research oriented faculty strives for quality education.
